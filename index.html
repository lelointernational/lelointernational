About Us

Lelo International is a diversified organization in India. It has headquarters in Durgapur, West Bengal. The journey of Lelo International started in the year 2011 with the motto of providing skill development to local youths and quality education to children. Gradually it has diversified its business portfolio to mineral trading, handicraft, corporate gifting, luxury lifestyle products, food & beverage products and many more.

Our vision is sustainable development across all our businesses.

Our Businesses

Coal Trading

Coal is a basic raw material for a diverse range of industries like infrastructure, energy, iron and steel, etc. Coal is mined in locations where it is found in abundance. However, this leads to a large distance gap between the place of demand and supply. Coal is generally required to be transported in enormous consignmentsdue to its dominant position as a vital raw material in the primary sector. ...We are engaged in coal trading and procuring coal from Coal India Limited, through its varioussubsidiaries viz; Eastern Coalfields Limited, Bharat Coking Coal Limited, Central Coalfields Limited, NorthernCoalfields Limited, South Eastern Coalfields Limited, Mahanadi Coalfields Limited, and NEC through “E-Auction”. TheCoal is being supplied to domestic industrial consumers including thermal power stations, brick fields &factories. We have developed necessary infrastructure & logistics support to deliver coal from the coal field to the doorstep of the customers with quality and quantity assurance in the time schedule stipulated.

Mineral Trading

We source base metals and ferro-alloys from the most reputed mines around India. We have direct tie-ups with producers and end-users and leverage our strengths of logistical support, competitive financing, risk management, quality assurance and timely delivery to ensure a high degree of customer satisfaction.

Handicrafts

India features amongst the topmost rated culturally rich countries in the world. The handicrafts of India have been loved and respected worldwide and have left everyone awestruck. Our mission is to uplift Indian handicraft society. We are making an ecosystem where artisans from rural and tribal areas are working together with new generation designers. We cater in varieties- Woodwork, Jute, Needle work, Leather, Pottery, Dhokra,Terracotta and many more.

Corporate Gifting

Our corporate gifting venture curates customized gifts for our clients. We deliver end to end gifting solutions. Receiving a gift is a powerful experience; it creates a sense of connection to the giver and can create positive associations with a person or a brand. Companies and marketers have long relied on the power of gifting to secure better relationships with potential and existing clients, to show appreciation for their business. When used as part of your overall marketing or employee retention efforts, gifting can be highly effective in terms of ROI and satisfaction
